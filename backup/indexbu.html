Gumball3000 CPM

The Gumball 3000 is an annual 3,000-mile international rally that blends cars, music, fashion, and entertainment. Originally founded by Maximillion Cooper in 1999 brought to the car parking multiplayer world by @cpmcoastcustoms and @sand3_moz — connecting car enthusiasts, creators, and gamers in one ultimate event. We have featured top players like SW Harry, CP Legends, Dukke, and Biaaanaraaa, showcasing the creativity, custom liveries, and the true spirit of the car culture community.
